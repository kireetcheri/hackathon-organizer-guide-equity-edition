# Social Media Kit

This kit gives you ready-to-post captions, a 4-week countdown schedule, and a day-of posting plan. Copy, fill in the brackets, and post.

**Platforms covered:** Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn (adapt tone as needed)

---

## 4-Week Countdown Schedule

### Week 4 — Announcement

**Goal:** Announce the event and drive early registrations.

**Instagram / Twitter caption:**

> 🚀 Announcing [HACKATHON NAME] — [DATE] at [LOCATION/SCHOOL].
>
> [N] hours. Build something real. Win prizes. No experience required.
>
> Registration is FREE → [link in bio / link]
>
> #hackathon #STEMeducation #[yourcity]hack #coding #highschool

**LinkedIn (more formal):**

> Excited to announce [HACKATHON NAME], a student-run hackathon at [SCHOOL] on [DATE].
>
> Open to [grades/ages]. Free registration. $[X] in prizes.
>
> We're also looking for sponsors and judges — DM me or reply here if you'd like to get involved.
>
> [Registration link]

---

### Week 3 — Social Proof / What to Expect

**Goal:** Reduce "I don't know if I'm good enough" friction.

> You don't need to know how to code to compete at [HACKATHON NAME].
>
> We'll have mentors, workshops, and snacks. Just bring your curiosity.
>
> [DATE] · [LOCATION] · FREE
> Register: [link]
>
> #hackathon #beginners #[school/club name]

**Story / Reel idea:** Film a 15-second "what is a hackathon?" explainer. Canva has free templates for this.

---

### Week 2 — Urgency + Logistics

**Goal:** Push fence-sitters to register. Share practical info.

> [HACKATHON NAME] is [N] days away and spots are filling up 👀
>
> 📅 [DATE]
> 📍 [LOCATION]
> ⏰ [START TIME] – [END TIME]
> 💸 Free to attend · $[X] in prizes
>
> Register before [DEADLINE]: [link]

**Separate post — FAQ format:**

> Quick answers for [HACKATHON NAME] 👇
>
> Q: Do I need a team?
> A: No, we'll help you find one.
>
> Q: What do I need to bring?
> A: Laptop, charger, ideas.
>
> Q: Is it really free?
> A: Yes. Food included.
>
> Register: [link]

---

### Week 1 / Final Push

**Goal:** Last call. Create FOMO.

> Last week to register for [HACKATHON NAME] 🔔
>
> [N] teams already signed up. [N] spots left.
>
> [DATE] · [LOCATION] · Free
> [link]

**Day before:**

> See you tomorrow, builders. 👋
>
> [HACKATHON NAME] starts at [TIME] at [LOCATION].
>
> Check-in opens at [TIME-30 min]. Bring your laptop and charger.
>
> Questions? DM us or email [contact email].

---

## Day-of Posting Plan

| Time | What to Post |
|------|-------------|
| Check-in opens | Photo of venue/setup: *"Doors are open. Let's build."* |
| 1 hour in | Action shot of teams working |
| Judging starts | *"Teams are presenting now — judges are deliberating 👀"* |
| Awards | Photo/video of winners with prizes |
| End of event | Group photo or collage + thank-you post |

**Day-of caption for awards:**

> 🏆 [HACKATHON NAME] winners:
>
> 🥇 [TEAM NAME] — [PROJECT NAME]: [1-sentence description]
> 🥈 [TEAM NAME] — [PROJECT NAME]: [1-sentence description]
> 🥉 [TEAM NAME] — [PROJECT NAME]: [1-sentence description]
>
> Thank you to our sponsors [tag them] and judges [tag if they have accounts].
>
> See you next year. 👋
>
> #hackathon #[eventname] #[yourcity]

## Hashtag Bank

Use 3-5 per post. Mix broad + specific.

**Broad (reach):**
`#hackathon` `#coding` `#buildinpublic` `#studenthackathon` `#STEM` `#womenintech`

**Location-specific (community):**
`#[CityName]tech` `#[CityName]students` `#[StateName]STEM`

**Event-specific (brand):**
`#[HackathonName]` `#[SchoolName]hacks`

---

## Repost Checklist

After the event, encourage participants to share:

- [ ] Post a link to your Devpost project page in your Instagram bio
- [ ] Tag the hackathon account when posting about what you built
- [ ] Use `#[HACKATHON NAME]` in posts

**Prompt you can send to all participants:**

> We'd love to see what you built! Share your project on social and tag us @[HANDLE] — we'll repost. Use #[HASHTAG].
